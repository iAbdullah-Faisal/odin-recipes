Description:

Cheesecake is a creamy, decadent dessert loved around the world. It features a buttery graham cracker crust and a smooth cream cheese filling that can be baked or chilled. You can top it with fruit compote, chocolate, or caramel for an indulgent finish that suits any celebration.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325¬∞F (163¬∞C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan to form the crust.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add the sugar and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until well combined.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  6. Mix in the sour cream and flour until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  7. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan, spreading it evenly.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 55-70 minutes, or until the center is almost set but still slightly jiggly.
  9. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the door slightly open for 1 hour to cool gradually.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
